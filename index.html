Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions) Page. If you have concerns that are not addressed here, please do get in touch with us via the Contact Us page. Thank you!

Order & Shipping

You can search and browse our collections for items that you might like in the navigation bar. Once you see an item you like, click on it to go to the item page. You can then click “Add to Cart” or “Buy Now” to take you to the checkout area where you can complete your order by inputting your shipping details and choosing a payment method.

Delivery times depend on your location. Within Legazpi City, orders typically arrive within 1–2 business days. For other areas in Bicol, allow 2–5 business days. For NCR and other regions, please allow 5–10 business days. You will receive a tracking number once your order has been dispatched.

Order changes can be made within 24 hours of placing your order. Once your order has been dispatched, we are unable to make any changes. Please contact us immediately via our Contact Us page or email if you need to modify your order.

We offer shipping via JRS Express and Grab for local deliveries. For pickup, you may visit any of our partner stores. Metro Manila deliveries are also available. Shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on your delivery address and the size/weight of your order.

Shipping is NOT included in the listing price. The shipping fee will be calculated and displayed at checkout based on your location and the items in your cart. You can also use our free shipping vouchers if applicable.

Payments, Returns, & Exchanges

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD) to pay in the latter date within our service area. Additional fees may vary on your delivery address and order value. COD appears as a payment option when you cannot fully pay online.

We accept GCash, online banking transfers, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for applicable areas. All payments are processed securely. For GCash payments, please send to our official account and include your order reference number.

Yes, we accept returns and refunds under specific conditions. Items must be returned within 7 days from date of purchase. Please refer to our Refund Policy for full details and eligibility criteria.

Refunds are typically processed within 5–10 business days from the date we receive and inspect the returned item. Refunds will be issued in the original form of payment. Please note that this will not include any shipping charges.

For made-to-order items, dispatch timelines depend on the information provided under the product page and confirmed at the checkout.

CHANGE OF DELIVERY ADDRESS. We are only able to change the delivery address before we issue you a shipping tracking code.

ORDER CANCELLATIONS. We are unable to accept cancellations after the order has been dispatched, except as otherwise provided under our Refund Policy.

SHIPPING INSURANCE. Packages are insured for loss and damage subject to the limits, exclusions, and terms of the courier assigned to your order.

  • You will see the calculated shipping fee on the checkout page and this fee will depend on the size and weight of the item(s) you ordered as well as the location of the delivery.
  • Larger packages may incur additional shipping charges and we shall inform you of this in an additional email if applicable. If the customer does not agree to the additional charge, the order may be cancelled prior to dispatch and refunded in accordance with our policies.
  • IMPORTANT: Please take note that for high-value items and/or if your address is located outside of the Philippines or in an area that requires relay shipping, there may be additional charges as determined by our courier partner. We shall also inform you of this in an additional email, if applicable, to confirm your agreement to pay for these additional charges.
  • Otherwise, other import duties and taxes for international shipments may be liable to be paid UPON arrival in your country. If you refuse to pay duties and taxes at your destination country, the goods will be returned to Souveniria but at your expense, and you will receive a refund for the value of the goods paid, minus the cost of the return shipping. The courier will be the official transporter of the goods.
  • Once you have fully paid for your order, we will provide you with a tracking code on your assigned courier.
  • In case of failure in delivery, the courier will make two (2) additional attempts to deliver to your address. If you're not present to receive the item, the order will be cancelled and you will be refunded the item price only (NOT the shipping fee that you paid for) as this is not considered a damaged or lost shipment under our Refund Policy.

  5. Used footwear, apparel (quality items or similar items)

Our Advocacy

“Every Souvenir Carries a Story”

Supporting the artisans and weavers of Bicol, one souvenir at a time.

Supporting Local Artisans

Souveniria was founded with a deep commitment to preserving Bicol’s rich cultural heritage. We work directly with local artisans, weavers, potters, and craftspeople across Albay, Camarines Sur, Sorsogon, and the wider Bicol region to bring their handcrafted creations to a national audience.

Sustainable & Ethical Sourcing

All our products are made using traditional, sustainable materials — abaca (laga), bamboo, pili shells, and natural dyes. We ensure fair trade pricing for every artisan partner, and a portion of every sale goes directly back to community livelihood programs in Legazpi, Daraga, and Tabaco.

Promoting Bicol Culture

From Mayon Volcano-inspired designs to Ibalong festival motifs, every Souveniria product is a wearable, giftable piece of Bicol’s story. We believe that souvenirs are more than mementos — they are ambassadors of culture, identity, and pride.

About Us

Souveniria
Bicol · Philippines

Founded with a vision rooted in local beginnings and deeply authentic support, Souveniria grew from a simple online showroom into a trusted local store through community-backed inspiration. We built a brand that deeply connects Bicolano craftsmanship with early buyers who valued direct handmade value rooted in the rich tradition and family love.

Souveniria stands as a home for local souvenirs where each product originates from community-based makers who apply artisanal skills, expert knowledge, and hours of processes to create items connected to culture, function, and place — allowing the store to serve as a cultural space where craftsmanship, identity, and livelihood connect through meaningful and sustainable business practices.

Home crafts and decor bring up some of the collection through woven baskets, wooden accents, natural fiber parrots, table items, tote items, and small household goods. Accessories, fashion quantities, where we connect to local aesthetics and practical details, span from wearables to bag collections. Garments including handmade textiles, varied cuts, accessories and hand-knitted pieces reflect regional accents, traditional techniques and modern craft from natural materials like abaca, raffia, bamboo or cotton blends.

Local suppliers advance their businesses through supported production schedules and forms. Souveniria with the store in many ways includes service, simple delivery, and convenient transact documents — helping farmers transform their locally produced pieces into local goods, and bring them closer to those who cherish respectful and transparent partnerships.
